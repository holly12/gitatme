Get At Who?

Hi there, I'm Neal Shyam and I like to build stuff.

I started git at -me because I like hacking on open source projects, but I was having trouble discovering projects that could use my help / interested me.

Every Thursday, git at -me highlights one handpicked oss project. I'll tell you what it is and why I dig it. After that, it's up to you.
